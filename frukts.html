peanuts

Technically, peanut belongs to the group of leguminous plants. Peanuts are rich in vitamin B9, which is necessary for the normal functioning of the nervous system, synthesis of DNA and RNA, and proper functioning of the brain. Eating peanuts in moderation stimulates brain activity, and protects the brain from age-related mental decline.
